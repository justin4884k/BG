Welcome

God Values You

At Rest Church, we believe "REST" is not passive — it's a dynamic, transformative experience. It empowers you to discover your purpose and fulfill your calling through divine strength.

God values you and has an incredible plan for your life. No matter where you're from or what you've been through, you are embraced, empowered, and equipped here.

Who We Are

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A church for all people, where everyone feels embraced, empowered, and equipped to transform their world through the Grace of the Trinity.

Our Mission

A welcoming home for all, rooted in the unconditional love of the Father, the finished work of the resurrected Christ, and the joy and fellowship of the Holy Spirit.

Our Community

We believe all believers comprise the priesthood, with the local church essential to empowering people across culture and establishing God's kingdom on earth.

Get Involved

Our Ministries

A place for every person — find your community and grow in your calling.

RC Kidz

Fun and safe age-appropriate Bible stories, worship, and prayer for children 0–12 years. Inspiring the next generation to change the world for Christ.

Ages 0–12 · Sundays 10 AM

RC Youth

High school and middle school students gathering for worship, prayer, and Christ-centered friendship. Helping students grow into the destiny God has in store.

Wednesdays 7 PM

Women of Rest

Empowering women to understand their identity in Christ. Monthly gatherings with shared meals, worship, ministry time, and one-on-one prayer.

Monthly · Saturdays

Men's Breakfast

Monthly gathering for men featuring fellowship, shared meals, one-on-one interaction, prayer, and uplifting biblical teaching. As iron sharpens iron.

Monthly · Saturdays 9 AM

EPIC Life Groups

Equipping People In Christ — weekly life groups throughout San Antonio for worship, prayer, Bible study, to equip, edify, and empower every believer.

Weekly · City-wide

RC Cares

Our outreach ministry dedicated to serving the San Antonio community with the love of Christ — meeting practical needs and sharing the good news.
